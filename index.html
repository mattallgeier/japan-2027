⚠️ Planning Notes

  • Ghibli Museum: Tickets sold by monthly lottery opening ~4 months before visit. Highest priority advance booking.
  • teamLab Planets: Book weeks ahead — popular, timed entry. Tauck likely handles this, confirm if independent.
  • Takarazuka Revue: Performance schedules released a few months out. A tour operator can help secure tickets.
  • Nara: Not on Tauck's itinerary — add as an independent half-day from Kyoto. Easily done by train.
  • Osaka: Tauck ends in Kyoto (Day 10 depart). Osaka and the aquarium require extending your trip by 1–2 days.
  • Miyajima deer: Free-roaming deer on the island — the kids will be delighted to encounter deer a second time after Nara.
  • June fireflies: A unique June-only experience at Kibune (north of Kyoto). Best 7–9pm. Combine with dinner at a riverside restaurant.
  • Sumo: June has no official tournament in Tokyo. Tauck likely arranges a private exhibition or stable morning visit instead.
  • Nijo Castle nightingale floor: The 9-year-old will especially enjoy this — the "squeaky floor" story is a great hook for kids.
  • Group split-up tip: Sumo / calligraphy / tea ceremony work well as adult-led while kids do a parallel activity.
